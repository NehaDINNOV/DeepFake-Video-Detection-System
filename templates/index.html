How it works

01

Frame extraction

Videos are split into evenly-spaced frames. Images are used directly. Each frame is resized to 299×299.

02

Dual-model ensemble

XceptionNet catches spatial blending artifacts. EfficientNet-B4 with a DCT branch catches GAN/diffusion frequency fingerprints.

03

Grad-CAM++ heatmaps

Gradient-weighted class activation maps reveal exactly which facial regions influenced the decision — eyes, hairline, skin boundaries.

04

LLaVA explanation

The original face and heatmap are sent to Ollama/LLaVA which provides a forensic plain-English explanation of the detected artifacts.
